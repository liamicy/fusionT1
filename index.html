Fusion is an all in one solution for schools and universities across the world offering an online or locally hosted VLE/LMS. Our service can be accessed via the web, and through our app which is available on iOS and Android..

Primary SECONDARY

Assessment & Grading

Set homework for your students and then mark it using the markbook. Students can upload submissions to the piece of homework or even put it in their workbook.

Multi-Lingual

In a diverse classroom it is important that every user can be a part of the online learning experience. This is why, Fusion can be used in over 90+ languages.

Customizable

Customize your dashboard to suit the needs of yourself. You can even set a default theme and layout for all your students and teachers.

MIS Integration

We are able to intergrate with your MIS system allowing us to bring in more information for both parens and staff such as a students timetable, attendance, progress reports and behaviour reports.

Website Builder

Users can create and build their very own websites using our custom Website Builder which allows your to create stunning websites with feature rich content such as videos, blog posts, files and much more!

Parent Portal

Allow parents to monitor their childs progress via our parent portal. Parents are able to see the homework their child has got as well as their scores and overall progress within lessons.
